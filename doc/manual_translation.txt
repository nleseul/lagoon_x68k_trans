In an age long ago, it was the first day of the 300th year. In the land of Lake Leland, darkness was beginning to spread. To restore peace to this land, seven mages gathered from its frontiers. Using ancient arts, they summoned forth God—or so they intended. But along with a gruesome dread, a demon raised its torso.
A tempest swept about them, and the mages' bodies were assailed by its rage. A darkness deeper than dark came into being there. The struggle between the mages and the demon was violent to the extreme, and it seemed that they would yield to the demon's mighty power.
However, using a forbidden ritual said to use one's allies as sacrifices, they were barely able to seal it. For this purpose, three of the seven were made sacrifices for the ritual, and only the four who remained—Mathias, Zerah, Duma, and Battler—joined together. For a brief, dreadful moment, their bodies were restrained. But a feeling of relief brought them back to consciousness, as they heard the voices of birds astir. However, within those relaxed expressions, did the others realize that there was one who cast a shadow of dark desire...?

This child, who had a gray right eye and a red left eye, was given the name "Thor." And from the moment his life in this world began, he was loathed by all people.

After Thor's birth, livestock were sickened and crops were poor, and his evil eyes were said to be the reason. Despite such tragic circumstances, he was given mysterious powers. Around his second birthday, he was able to speak perfectly, and he was also able to predict whatever would happen on the next day. But such powers only led to more fear from the humans around him. Nassel was raised a lively and kindhearted youth. At an early age, he was introduced to swordsmanship using the blades made by his father, and already displayed prodigious skill. 
One day, a monk visited Nassel's house. The monk introduced himself as Mathias. He said that Nassel was undoubtedly the hero of the Moonblade, and that he intended Nassel's father Roy to create the Moonblade. The Legend of the Moonblade

ムーンブレイドは羽のように軽くなり、主人
の意のままに動き、魔法の光を発するのだ。
しかし、ムーンブレイドが主人と認めない者

　伝説によれば、ムーンブレイドの勇者は、

誕生から、ちょうど1年後に生まれ、いずれ
は闇と戦うことになるという。
Many ages ago, a fragment of a holy sword used in the wars of the gods fell to earth, became an ore, and spent a long time within the earth, their true nature concealed. 
This ore possessed magical powers, and a sword forged from the ore would be the only weapon to fight against an evil god. 
Everyone was obsessed with the allure of the sword's legend and sought out the ore, but not one of them was able to return with them. 
Regardless of their veracity, in stories told by humans who said they had seen it, the ore was said to glow milky white, beautiful like the reflection of the moon on a lake. Because of those stories, the stone came to be called the Moonstones, and the sword, the Moonblade, Taking the Moonblade is possible for only one person, a chosen hero. In the hands of the hero, the Moonblade will become feather-light, moving as its master wills, and emitting a magical light. However, if a person the Moonblade does not recognize as its master grasps its hilt, it would become suddenly heavy as though it had transformed into the whole earth, and that person would be shown strange illusions. 
According to the legend, the hero of the Moonblade will have, at the time of birth, a mark in the shape of the moon on their right shoulder. The hero will be born exactly one year after the one who rules the darkness, and in time will battle the darkness. 

王家の血筋
The Royal Lineage

　レイクリーランドの王家には、ラグーン城を
移動させる力が備わっている。特に第一皇女に
は、その血が色濃く受け継がれている。だがそ
の力を、国が危機に頻した際の非常手段以外に
は使用してはならないという掟がある。
そして、その力を行使するにあたっては、 皇女
の意志のもとに行われなくてはならないという。
なぜなら、ラグーン城から湧きだす水は、域が
地上から離れたとたんに輝きを失い、臣民達の
生活を危うくするために他ならない。
The royal family of Lake Leland possess the power to move Lagoon Castle. In particular, that blood is markedly passed down to the firstborn princess. However, there is a law that says this power should not be used except in emergency situations of repeated crises to the kingdom. 
And, it says that the use of this power should not be valid except by the will of the princess. This is because the water that springs from Lagoon Castle loses its radiance as soon as the castle separates from the ground, and the well-being of her subjects should not be endangered.
